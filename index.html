Google Sign-In Setup Required

To enable real Google Sign-In, you need to add your Google Client ID. This takes about 5 minutes in Google Cloud Console.

Quick Steps
  1. Open console.cloud.google.com
  2. APIs & Services → Credentials → Create OAuth 2.0 Client
  3. Copy your Client ID
  4. Paste it in backend/.env as GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID
  5. Restart the server → Click Google Sign-In again
